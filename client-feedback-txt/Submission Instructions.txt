Submission Instructions

Follow these steps carefully to submit your appeal to Amazon Seller Performance:

1Copy or Download: Use the buttons above to copy your appeal text or download it as a formatted PDF document.

2Access Seller Central: Log in to your Amazon Seller Central account at sellercentral.amazon.com

3Navigate to Appeals: Go to Performance → Account Health → Appeals or check your Performance Notifications.  If this option is not available to you, open a case with Seller Support and submit the POA as an attachment .

4Paste Your Appeal: Copy your appeal letter and paste it into the appropriate text field provided by Amazon

5Attach Documents: Upload all supporting documents you prepared (invoices, certificates, IDs, etc.) as required by your suspension notice

6Submit & Monitor: Submit your appeal and monitor your email for Amazon's response. Typical response time is 24-48 hours, though complex cases may take longer
